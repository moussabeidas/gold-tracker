Privacy Policy

Gold Pricer · Last updated July 4, 2026

The short version: your portfolio never leaves your device. Gold Pricer has no server of its own, collects no analytics, shows no ads, and does not track you.

Information stored on your device

  • Portfolio data — the gold purchases you record (name, weight, price paid, dates, notes, and photos) are stored only in the app's local storage on your iPhone. They are never uploaded anywhere.
  • Sign in with Apple — when you sign in, Apple provides an anonymous user identifier and, at your choice, your name and email. These are kept in your device's secure keychain solely to associate your portfolio with you on that device. We have no server and never receive them.
  • Photos — pictures you take of your gold, and the on-device text recognition used to read their engravings, are processed entirely on your iPhone. Images are not transmitted.

Information requested from third parties

To show live prices, charts, and news, the app requests public market data from third-party services (a gold spot-price API and Yahoo Finance endpoints). Like any internet request, these services receive your IP address when contacted. No personal or portfolio information is included in these requests.

What we don't do

  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No tracking across apps or websites
  • No selling or sharing of data — we never possess it in the first place

Data deletion

Deleting the app removes all portfolio data and sign-in information from your device. You can also revoke the app's Sign in with Apple access at any time in Settings → Apple ID → Sign-In & Security.

Children

Gold Pricer is not directed at children under 13 and collects no personal information from anyone.
