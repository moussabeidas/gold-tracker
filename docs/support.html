Common questions

Where is my portfolio stored? On your device. Deleting the app deletes your portfolio.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription? Subscriptions are managed by Apple: Settings → your name → Subscriptions, or in the app under Profile → Manage or Cancel Pro.

How do I restore a purchase on a new phone? Open the upgrade screen and tap "Restore Purchases".

Widgets show no data? Open the app once after installing — it hands the latest prices to the widgets.
